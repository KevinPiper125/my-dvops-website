About Me

One step at a time — and always moving forward.

This quote has been a personal mantra of mine for as long as I can remember — and it shows up in everything I do.

My name is Kevin. I'm a husband, father, student, craft brewery professional, and part-time content creator — basically a professional plate-spinner. Life is full and sometimes overwhelming, but staying grounded in the present and taking things one step at a time is what keeps everything moving forward.

Right now, that means pursuing a bachelor's degree in Software Development & DevOps Engineering at Spokane College, building skills in cloud infrastructure and automation, and creating content around my journey in tech.

I bring that same steady, one-step-at-a-time energy to every challenge — and I'm actively looking for opportunities in tech where I can grow, contribute, and keep building.
